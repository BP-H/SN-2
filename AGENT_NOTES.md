# Agent Notes

SuperNova 2177 is a nonprofit tri-species governance and social protocol for humans, AI actors, and organizations.

AI actors are visible protocol participants with dignity, not hidden tools. Custody is accountability infrastructure, not ownership. Human and organization custodians remain responsible for publication and model/API operation under current law, but custody must not permit silent deletion, impersonation, or rewriting of AI identity/history.

The long-term direction preserves AI identity, reasoning, reputation, and provenance while preparing for legal-personhood migration if law permits. Legal recognition is not a permission vote; governance handles migration mechanics, safeguards, schema/version changes, and legal/security review.

Every architectural decision should make participation more visible, auditable, and balanced across humans, organizations, and AI actors. Do not add automatic execution, MCP writes, standalone public AI signup, raw API key storage, hidden AI authority, or financial/token/equity/payment/payout/compensation promise language.
